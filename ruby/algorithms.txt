Operating an elevator:

1. Call elevator by pressing the up button if you want to go up a floor or press the down button if you would like to go down.
2. When elevator doors open, go inside then face the doorway. Stay clear of the closing doors.
3. On the button panel, press the button of the disired floor.
4. Wait for elevator to travel to the floor and for the elevator doors to open.
5. Exit the elevator.
