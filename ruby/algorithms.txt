Operating an elevator:

1. Call the elevator by pressing the up button if you want to go up a floor or press the down button if you would like to go down.
2. When elevator doors open, go inside then face the doorway. 
3. Stay clear of the closing doors.
4. Decide which floor you would like to travel to.
5. On the button panel, the buttons are numbers that represent which floor the elevator will travel to. Press your desired floor number.
4. Wait for elevator to travel to the floor and the elevator doors will open to your desired floor.
5. Exit the elevator.

How to brush your teeth

1. Hold your toothbrush in your dominant hand with the brush head in the same direction as your thumb with the brushes facing up.
2. Squeeze half an inch of toothpaste onto the brushes of the toothbrush.
3. Set the toothbrush on your front surface of your teeth so that the toothpaste is applied to your teeth.
4. Beginning with the front of your upper teeth, brush your teeth in a horizontal back and forth motion with light pressure for 30 seconds. Do not swallow toothpaste.
5. Repeat step 4 on front lower teeth.
6. Repeat step 4 on back lower teeth.
7. Repeat step 4 on back upper teeth.
8. Spit toothpaste into the sink.
9. Take a sip of water into your mouth and swiss water around and then spit into the sink again. Repeat as needed.
10. Rinse off toothbrush.
